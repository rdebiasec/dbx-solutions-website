# Section Q&A — Security Roadmap

## Overview

**Q: What is the Security Roadmap page?**  
A: An honest view of security and compliance work—what is planned or in progress, not claimed as finished unless formally obtained.

**Q: Are you SOC 2 certified today?**  
A: SOC 2 Type I and Type II appear on our roadmap as readiness work—not as completed certification unless we have formally obtained and published it.

---

## Roadmap items

**Q: What is on your security roadmap?**  
A: SOC 2 Type I readiness, SOC 2 Type II future readiness, ISO/IEC 27001 alignment, Data Processing Agreement readiness, Privacy Policy and Terms, internal data handling procedures, Responsible AI policy, and vendor security review process.

**Q: Why list things that are not done yet?**  
A: So you know what is planned vs. complete. We document in progress work transparently.

---

## For prospects

**Q: Is my data secure if you are still working on certifications?**  
A: We align operations with recognized practices and use reasonable safeguards. Certifications on the roadmap are goals—we do not claim badges we have not earned.

**Q: Can we get a DPA?**  
A: Data Processing Agreement readiness is on our roadmap. Ask in a consultation for current status.

**Q: Do you review vendor security?**  
A: Yes—a vendor security review process is part of our roadmap and operations approach.

---

## Disclaimer

**Q: Should I treat roadmap items as completed certifications?**  
A: No. Certifications listed are roadmap items only unless we explicitly state they have been formally obtained and published.
