## High-priority sectors

**Q: Which industries are high priority for DBX?**  
A: Professional services, healthcare and wellness clinics, real estate teams, and local service businesses.

**Q: Professional services — what is a typical use case?**  
A: Collect project scope on WhatsApp, qualify inquiries faster, hand off to the right advisor with full context.

**Q: Healthcare / clinics — what can you help with?**  
A: After-hours questions about hours and services, guide booking inquiries, escalate urgent cases to staff. AI supports communication—not clinical judgment.

**Q: Real estate — example?**  
A: Qualify buyers/sellers on WhatsApp, answer property FAQs, alert the listing advisor with budget and timing.

**Q: Local services — example?**  
A: Collect quote details (service type, location, photos) so your team replies with accurate next steps.

## Additional sectors

**Q: What other industries do you cover?**  
A: E-commerce, education and training, and financial/advisory services.

**Q: E-commerce example?**  
A: Order-status questions on WhatsApp with structured details before a human handles exceptions.

**Q: Education example?**  
A: Program FAQs on chat; enrollment-ready leads land in your system with notes.

**Q: Financial / advisory example?**

## Regulated sectors

**Q: Can regulated businesses use this?**  
A: AI can support communication efficiency. It does not replace professional advice, compliance review, clinical judgment, legal judgment, or required human oversight. See Responsible AI Policy.

**Q: Is there a one-size-fits-all template per industry?**  
A: No—examples on this page are starting points; we design around your business.
