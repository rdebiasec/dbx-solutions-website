## Page hero

**Q: What should I contact you about?**  
A: Where WhatsApp and customer conversations are costing you time or leads—we help identify the most practical first improvement.

**Q: What is the fastest way to get a response?**  
A: Book directly on the calendar. The form works for a short note if you prefer.

---

## Consultation options

**Q: How do I book a consultation?**  
A: Use the Google Calendar link on the site, or email contact@dbx-solutions.com.

**Q: What happens when I submit the contact form?**  
A: The form opens your email client with your details (mailto flow). We follow up after we receive it.

**Q: What fields are on the form?**  
A: Name, business email (required), phone or WhatsApp, what you want to improve (required dropdown), optional message.

**Q: What can I select under "What are you looking to improve?"**  
A: Slow replies on WhatsApp, leads not followed up, same questions all day, info stuck in chats, or not sure where to start.

---

## SMS on contact form

**Q: Do I have to agree to SMS to submit the form?**  
A: Only if you enter a phone number—you must check the SMS consent box in that case. Otherwise it is not required.

**Q: What does the SMS consent checkbox say?**  
A: You agree to automated texts from DBX about appointments, services, and support. Reply STOP to opt out, HELP for help. Message/data rates may apply. Consent is not required to buy.

---

## Privacy on contact

**Q: What happens to my form data?**  
A: By submitting, you agree DBX may process your information to respond, as described in the Privacy Policy. The form sends by email and is not stored on our servers.

---

## Pricing

**Q: How much does it cost?**  
A: Pricing is not listed on the site. Scope and cost depend on your situation—we discuss that in a consultation after understanding your channels and goals.
