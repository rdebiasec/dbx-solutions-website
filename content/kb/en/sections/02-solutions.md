## What we help with (five areas)

**Q: How many solution areas do you list on this page?**  
A: Five practical ways—each starts from a real customer moment, not a technology catalog.

**Q: What is solution #1 — Reply on WhatsApp and chat?**  
A: Answer common questions fast, even after hours. Your team takes over when a person needs to decide. Good if customers write you and answers take too long.

**Q: What is solution #2 — Sort leads before follow-up?**  
A: Ask the right questions first so your team calls back with context. Good if inquiries feel messy.

**Q: What is solution #3 — Repeat questions?**  
A: Common support questions get a consistent answer; tricky cases go to a person. Good if your team answers the same things all day.

**Q: What is solution #4 — Update records without retyping?**  
A: What the customer said shows up in your customer system, calendar, or team alerts automatically. Good if info stays stuck in chats.

**Q: What is solution #5 — Where to start with AI?**  
A: We look at how customers reach you and pick practical first steps—not a big vague project. Good if you want AI help but do not know what to do first.
