## § The Challenge (`kb.home.challenge` · `#challenge`)

**Q: What problem are you describing?**  
A: Customer demand grows faster than manual follow-up. Conversations and next steps get spread across busy people and disconnected tools.

**Q: What are the six pain points you list?**  
A: Slow responses, repetitive questions, missed leads, disconnected tools, limited team capacity, and customer frustration from inconsistent replies.

**Q: We are not lazy—why is follow-up still hard?**  
A: The issue is usually not effort. It is that messages, customer details, and next steps live in different places and channels.

**Q: How does DBX fix this in simple terms?**
