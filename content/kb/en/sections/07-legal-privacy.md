## Overview

**Q: What does your Privacy Policy cover?**  
A: How we collect, use, share, and keep information when you visit our site, submit an inquiry, book a consultation, or contact us.

**Q: Who is the company behind the policy?**  
A: DBX Solutions LLC, a Florida limited liability company.

## What we collect

**Q: What information might you collect from me?**  
A: Name, email, phone, company details, inquiry content—and automatically things like IP address, browser type, pages visited, if you use the website.

**Q: Do you collect communications?**  
A: We may keep records when you email, call, text, or use on-site messaging tools.

---

## How we use it

**Q: Why do you use my information?**  
A: To respond to you, schedule consultations, send service-related messages, improve the site, run our business safely, and meet legal obligations.

**Q: Do you sell my personal information?**  
A: No. We do not sell personal information.

---

## SMS & cookies

**Q: Will you text me?**  
A: Only if you provide a phone number and consent where required—for things like appointment reminders or service updates. Reply STOP to opt out.

**Q: Do you use cookies?**  
A: Yes, to understand site usage and improve performance. You can control cookies in your browser settings.

## Sharing & subprocessors

**Q: Who do you share data with?**  
A: Service providers that help us operate—hosting, analytics, messaging (e.g. Intercom), fonts (Google Fonts), email/infrastructure. They work on our behalf.

**Q: Is data stored only in the U.S.?**  
A: We are U.S.-based; data may be processed in the U.S. and other countries where we or providers operate.

---

## Your rights

**Q: Can I ask you to delete or correct my data?**  
A: Yes—contact us to request access, correction, or deletion where applicable, or to opt out of non-essential messages.

**Q: I am in California—do I have CPRA rights?**  
A: California residents may have rights to know, delete, correct, and opt out of certain sharing. We do not sell personal information.

**Q: I am in Colombia—what are my rights?**  
A: Under Law 1581 of 2012 you may know, update, rectify, delete data, revoke consent, and complain to the SIC. Contact us for requests (10 business days for inquiries, 15 for claims).

**Q: I am in Brazil—what about LGPD?**  
A: See the Portuguese Privacy Policy section on LGPD rights (access, correction, deletion, portability, etc.) and ANPD complaints.
