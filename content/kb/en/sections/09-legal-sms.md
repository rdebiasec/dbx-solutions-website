## Program

**Q: What SMS messages might I receive from DBX?**  
A: Automated texts related to your business interaction—appointment reminders, consultation coordination, support, or service updates.

**Q: Do you send spam or buy phone lists?**  
A: No unsolicited messages and no purchased or rented contact lists.

---

## Opt-in & opt-out

**Q: How do I agree to receive texts?**  
A: Check the SMS consent box on our form (or provide your number and affirm consent where required). Consent is not required to purchase services.

**Q: How do I stop texts?**  
A: Reply **STOP** to any message. You may get one final confirmation.

**Q: How do I get help with SMS?**  
A: Reply **HELP** or contact contact@dbx-solutions.com / +1 (321) 287-4509.

---

## Costs & delivery

**Q: Are text messages free?**  
A: Message and data rates from your carrier may apply. DBX does not charge those fees.

**Q: What if a text does not arrive?**  
A: Delivery depends on carriers and third-party providers. DBX is not responsible for carrier delays or failures.

---

## Privacy

**Q: Do you share my mobile number for marketing?**  
A: No—we do not share mobile information with third parties or affiliates for their own marketing.

**Q: Where is SMS data handled?**  
A: Per our Privacy Policy at dbx-solutions.com/privacy-policy/
