## Page hero

**Q: What is the Services page about?**  
A: How we make WhatsApp and customer help practical—from chat replies to syncing with your tools and supporting you after launch.

**Q: How is Services different from Solutions?**  
A: Solutions describes *what* we help with; Services describes *how we implement* it around how your team already works.

## All six services (full detail)

**Q: List all services on the Services page.**  
A: (1) Reply on WhatsApp and chat, (2) Sort leads before follow-up, (3) Handle repeat questions, (4) Update CRM without retyping, (5) Know where to start with AI, (6) Keep improving it with you.

**Q: Which services have a "Start here" badge?**  
A: WhatsApp/chat replies and lead sorting.

**Q: What does the ongoing service ("We run it with you") include?**  
A: After launch we review real conversations and adjust so it stays useful—not a one-time setup that goes stale.

**Q: Do most clients buy all six at once?**  
A: Most teams start with one channel or one problem, not a big project.
