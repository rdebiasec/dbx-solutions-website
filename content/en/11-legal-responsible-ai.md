## Working principles

1. Accuracy and clarity over speed alone
2. Human escalation for sensitive, uncertain, or high-stakes requests
3. Clear limits on what AI can answer or act on
4. Transparent use of approved business information
5. Continuous improvement based on real customer interactions

---

## Regulated industries

For healthcare, financial services, legal, insurance, and other regulated contexts, AI supports communication and workflow efficiency. It does not replace professional advice, compliance review, clinical judgment, legal judgment, or required human oversight.
