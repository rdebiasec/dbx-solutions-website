## Our approach

DBX Solutions builds data handling practices for client implementations—approved content sources, controlled access, and secure integration planning. This notice covers project work, not website privacy (see Privacy Policy).

---

## What we prioritize

1. Using approved business content as the source for customer-facing answers
2. Limiting access to systems and data required for each customer flow
3. Designing integrations so useful context reaches your CRM or team—not open-ended data exposure
4. Documenting what is processed, stored, and retained per project scope
5. Aligning with your privacy policy, SMS terms, and industry requirements where applicable
