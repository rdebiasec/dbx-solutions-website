# Customer FAQs (English)

Answers in plain language. If someone wants technical detail (integrations, AI architecture, compliance frameworks), offer that only when they ask.

## What DBX does

**What is DBX Solutions?**  
We help growing businesses answer customers faster—especially on WhatsApp—and keep follow-up organized so your team does not lose leads or repeat the same answers all day.

**Is this just a chatbot?**  
No. We set up helpful first replies using your real business info (prices, hours, services), but your team can step in anytime—especially when a human decision is needed.

**Who is this for?**  
Small and mid-sized businesses that get customer messages on WhatsApp, chat, email, or SMS and want faster replies without hiring a bigger team.

**What problem do you actually solve?**  
Slow replies, missed leads, the same questions over and over, and customer details stuck in chats instead of where your team works.

## WhatsApp & channels

**Can you work with WhatsApp?**  
Yes. WhatsApp is often the main channel we start with. We can also work with web chat, email, SMS, and forms—depending on how your customers already reach you.

**Do customers have to use a new app or portal?**  
No. They keep messaging you the way they already do. We improve what happens on your side after they write.

**What if we use more than one channel?**  
That is common. We look at where most conversations start and help connect the dots so your team sees what was said, no matter the channel.

**Will replies sound like our business?**  
Yes. Answers are built from information you approve—your wording, your prices, your policies—not generic filler.

## Services & daily help

**What can DBX help with first?**  
Most teams start with one of these:
- Faster replies on WhatsApp or chat (even after hours)
- Collecting lead details before your team calls back
- Handling repeat questions so staff focus on harder cases
- Getting chat details into your customer records without retyping

**We get inquiries but follow-up is messy. Can you help?**  
Yes. We ask the right questions upfront so when your team follows up, they already know what the customer needs.

**Our team answers the same questions all day. Sound familiar?**  
We handle the common ones consistently. Unusual or sensitive cases go to a person on your team.

**We want help with AI but do not know where to start.**  
We review how customers reach you today and recommend one practical first step—not a huge vague project.

## Your team stays in control

**Will this replace our customer service team?**  
No. The goal is to support your team—less repetitive work, faster first responses, and people free for conversations that need judgment.

**Can a real person take over a chat?**  
Yes. We build clear handoff paths so your team can review, reply, or take over whenever it matters.

**What if the system gives a wrong answer?**  
We use your approved information, clear rules, and ongoing review of real conversations to catch and fix issues. Your team stays in the loop.

**Do we lose control after launch?**  
No. You set the rules. We help you review chats and update answers as your business changes.

## Getting started & timeline

**How do we get started?**  
Book a short consultation. We review your channels, find the first improvement to make, and outline practical next steps—no pressure.

**How long does setup take?**  
It depends on scope. Many businesses start with one focused use case (one channel, one type of question) and expand after that works.

**What does your process look like?**  
Four simple phases:
1. **Discover** — Understand how customers reach you and where time is lost  
2. **Design** — Map replies, handoffs, and what success looks like  
3. **Implement** — Build, test, and launch on your channels  
4. **Optimize** — Improve based on real conversations

**Do we need a big IT project?**  
No. We focus on practical steps sized for growing businesses—not enterprise-style rollouts.

**Where do you work with clients?**  
Remotely across the U.S. and Latin America.

## Who it's for (industries)

**What types of businesses do you work with?**  
Especially businesses where fast replies and good follow-up drive revenue—professional services, clinics, real estate, local services, e-commerce, education, and advisory firms.

**We're a professional services firm. Does this fit?**  
Often yes—for example, collecting project scope on WhatsApp and routing qualified inquiries to the right person with full context.

**We're a clinic or wellness practice.**  
We can help with after-hours questions about hours and services, guide booking inquiries, and escalate urgent cases to staff.

**We're in healthcare, finance, or legal. Is AI safe for us?**  
AI can help with routine communication and workflow—but it does not replace professional advice, clinical judgment, legal judgment, or required human review. Sensitive topics go to your team.
